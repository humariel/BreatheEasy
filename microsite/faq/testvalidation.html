Test and Validation

Here we present tests made to the system and their respective results.

Chosen Technologies

2/04/19

For now, the thecnologies chosen by the group have not been an imparement for the project to move on. The first big functionality was successfully implemented (the exchange of data to the client side in the form of graphics).

Our Personas

Since the system is still in an initial state, tests with our personas haven't taken place yet, but the group plans on testing the system with all our personas and use their feedback to hopeully upgrade our interfaces and project in general.

27/05/19

On this date, two members of the group (André Mourato & Gonçalo Vítor) met with one of our personas, an environmental engineering student, Vera Rodrigues. She liked how we chose to show information on the map (in small hexagons). She was also very helpful and thought us what main pollutants people that know about air quality look to know about, the units in which they are measured and common scales for them. With her help we ended up integrating other sources of data to gather information on air pollutants.

Our Standarts

The group worked with an environment engineer to better understand how to evaluate air quality and follows the stadarts set by the European Commission on limits of pollutant gases in the atmosphere.

Our Data

The group gathers data from real sensors that where integrated in our project through partnerships with other projects, from PEI course and IT. But the ammount of sensors aquired is limited. To show the power of our product we need as much data as possible. Having hundreds, maybe thousands of devices is something that requires costs we can't support on our own. To overcome this problem the group decided to gather data from two external API's, Dark Sky and BreezoMeter, from which we can retrieve a larger ammount of data and display our platform's potential.
