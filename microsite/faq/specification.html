Specification Area

In this section we'll give an updated insight of what our service should be able to provide.

First Demo Stage

We are still on an early stage of the project. For now, the main core functionality is implemented and the system should now be able to provide users visual graphics of basic data like temperature, wind and waste information relative to Aveiro city.

Second Demo Stage

By now, the team has been able to integrate several sources of data, which was one the team's big goals. We've made colaborations with both other projects from PEI course as well as external projects. We are working on improving our data visualization at the moment and expect to show results at the full demo stage.

Full Demo Stage

We have changed the way the information is displayed on the map by dividing Aveiro's district into smaller pieces (hexagons). After a meeting with one of our personas and also changed what information is displayed on screen. To be specific, we added concentration values of the most common/concerning air pollutants. We also added an alert system that notifies people when anything possibly hazardous happens (like the temperature being too high). The alert system also allows people to configure situations they want to be notified about.
